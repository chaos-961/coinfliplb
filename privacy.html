Privacy Policy

Coinflip LB collects only the information needed to operate the game account and keep the service secure.

Information used

  • Username and password authentication data.
  • Session token stored in your browser session storage.
  • Game-round activity, virtual Fun Gold balance, and leaderboard stats.
  • Technical security data such as IP/user-agent hashes handled by the backend for abuse prevention.

No payment data

The frontend does not collect payment cards, bank details, crypto wallets, deposits, withdrawals, or prize-redemption information.

Cookies and storage

The app uses browser storage for your session token, theme preference, and local notification state.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the site operator through the official contact channel for Coinflip LB.
