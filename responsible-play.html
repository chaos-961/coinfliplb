Responsible Play

Coinflip LB is designed as a casual entertainment game using virtual Fun Gold only. It should not be treated as a way to make money or win prizes.

Healthy-use reminders

  • Play only for entertainment.
  • Take breaks often.
  • Stop if the game feels stressful or frustrating.
  • Do not use the game as a substitute for real-money gambling.

No purchase, cashout, or prize

The service does not accept deposits and does not allow withdrawals. Fun Gold has no monetary value.

Need help?

If games of chance are causing distress, consider speaking with someone you trust or contacting a local gambling-support organization in your country.
